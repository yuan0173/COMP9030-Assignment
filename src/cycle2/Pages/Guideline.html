How to use this website

The following guidelines help ensure respectful, safe, and effective use of the Indigenous Art Atlas.

General Use

  • Browse artwork listings and map locations
  • Filter and search using categories like type, state, and period
  • View detailed descriptions, comments, and related media

Submitting Artwork

  • Create an account to contribute new entries
  • Provide accurate details (title, type, description, period, etc.)
  • Upload clear images and include location information

Respect Protocols

Do not share sensitive or sacred site information unless authorized. Respect privacy and cultural ownership.

Community Standards

Submissions must avoid offensive content. Moderators may remove entries that violate community guidelines.
