About Us

The Indigenous Art Atlas is a community-driven database for tracking and sharing Indigenous art in real-world settings — from ancient cave art to contemporary galleries and public installations.

Our Mission

Preserve cultural heritage, support artists and communities, and promote respectful discovery through open, accessible information.

What You’ll Find

  • Artwork listings with context and location
  • Submission portal for community contributions
  • Guidelines on cultural sensitivity and privacy

Respect & Sensitivity

We follow protocols for culturally sensitive and private locations. Please read the Guidelines before submitting.

How to Contribute

Create an account to submit artworks. Provide clear descriptions, context, and note any sensitivities or permissions.
